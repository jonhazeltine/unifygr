OUR VISIONENCOUNTER GODWe want to create an atmosphere where the Holy Spirit rests. We invite every member to engage in making this a reality in our gatherings. The Presence of God is attracted:Through desire (James 4:8)Through worship (2 Chronicles 5:13-14/Psalm 22:3)Through obedience and service (John 12:26)Through humility (James 4:10)Through unity (2 Chronicles 15:12/2 Chronicles 15:15)Through prayer and generosity (Acts 10:2-4)BE TRANSFORMEDWe believe that personal transformation is possible! New Life is building ministries that assist in personal transformation. We also believe that corporate transformation is possible. We are continually re-evalutating our culture, our environment and our behavior by asking the question, "How can we improve so that more glory will be given to God?"CHANGE THE WORLDWe believe that it is the calling of every believer to participate in bringing the light of heaven into every area of society:BusinessGovernmentFamilyReligion MediaEducationEntertainmentAnd to participate in the Great Commission by seeing the news of God's kindness spread throughout the world (Matthew 28:19-20).
OUR VALUESPASSION FOR JESUSAn insatiable desire for an intimate and growing relationship with Christ.TRANSPARENT RELATIONSHIPSIntentionally and openly connecting with God and others.SUPERNATURAL LIFEAccepting all of God's gifts and allowing for the Holy Spirit to minister powerfully and miraculously through us.GODLY CHARACTERA firm commitment to choose what is right in all that we do.PASSION FOR PEOPLEJoyfully pouring out the Father's Love and Blessing in our Church, in our Community and around the World.
